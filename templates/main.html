Crypto Cheques are basically paper wallets with a convenient way of redeeming. For redeeming bitcoins from a cheque the cheque ID as well as the verification information on the paper cheque are necessarily needed (we store each private key encrypted based on this information).

PLEASE NOTE: It is not possible to recover any bitcoins if the information printed on the cheque is lost!

Payout fees: 1.5% service fee + bitcoin network fee

Crypto-Cheque is open source: https://github.com/hroser/crypto-cheque

If there are any other issues with Crypto Cheque, please contact us: info@crypto-cheque.com
